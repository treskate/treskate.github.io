"Ez7 Montage"

I finally got around to making another montage. This is most of the clips I filmed in the past year or so at the ez7 skatepark. I had a lot more footage, but it was all filmed with a standard definition camcorder (dvx100b) so I couldn't mix it. Everything was filmed with a Canon HV30 and 5D Mark II. All the clips are 30p. I have mix feelings about the song I picked, but I wanted to use something that captured the feel of the ez7 skatepark. Which is, to me, a chill place to skate and have fun. Plus the song is called "Easy" so I pretty much had to. Hate it or love it. Either way you're reading this and hopefully watched the video. I thank you.
